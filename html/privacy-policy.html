Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At LogoFlux, we collect information necessary to deliver custom brand design and motion graphics services. This includes:

  • Personal Contact Data: Name and email address provided in briefing forms.
  • Project Details: Brand names, styling requests, logo vector files, and animations ideas.
  • Payment Data: Secure billing transaction details processed by Stripe or Paddle (we do not store credit card details locally).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to fulfill your project instructions, including:

  • Creating and animating custom logo concepts.
  • Communicating updates, drafts, revision reviews, and deliveries.
  • Verifying transaction authenticity and providing secure downloads of your package source files.

3. Data Security & Storage

We implement advanced technical protocols and industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal details and custom assets against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Design source files are kept in secure backups for client support purposes and are never shared with third parties.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We utilize minor essential cookies to analyze site traffic, store session states, and improve your overall navigation experience. You can manage your cookie preferences directly in your browser settings.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions about our data practices or want to request the deletion of your personal details, email us at hello@logoflux.com.
